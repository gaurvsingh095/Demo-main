How It Works

Our AI-powered pipeline automates every step of your job application:

  1. Upload your existing resume (PDF, DOCX).
  2. Scrape your target job posting URL—gather responsibilities, requirements & skills.
  3. Analyze gaps and perfect matches between your profile and the role.
  4. Tailor your resume bullets and summary for laser-focused alignment.
  5. Generate a polished cover letter and a courteous follow-up email with one click.

Finally, we package everything into a ZIP for instant download or email integration—so you apply in seconds.
